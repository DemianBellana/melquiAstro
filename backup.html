About Saffron

Hi, I'm Saffron

I'm a wedding and lifestyle photographer based in Truro, NS, but always ready to travel wherever your story takes me. In 2019, I took a leap and started this business — mostly by accident.

"My style is all about capturing fleeting moments — the in-between glances, the deep belly laughs, the way the light catches just right."

I'm easygoing, down-to-earth, and always up for an adventure — whether that means hiking to a breathtaking elopement spot or running barefoot through a golden field at sunset.
